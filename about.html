Hungry Fellas History

  • 2008

    Hungry Fellas idea is founded by three hungry students who were looking for an alternative way to order foods.

  • 2010

    By the time, that three students officialy formed Hungry Fellas company in Brisbane Australia.

  • 2011

    They launched the first Hungry Fellas comercial website.

  • 2013

    Almost all of the restaurants in University of Queensland joined Hungry Fellas.

  • 2015

    Hungry Fellas was processing over 2000 orders per month.
